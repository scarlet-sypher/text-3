Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new project?

Go to the Projects tab and click on the "New Project" button. Fill in the project details and save.

How do I assign a bug to a team member?

Open the bug details, click on the "Assign" dropdown, and select the team member from the list.

How do I change my notification settings?

Go to Settings > Notification Settings and adjust your preferences according to your needs.

How do I export bug reports?

In the Reports section, select the criteria for your report, then click the "Export" button to download as CSV or PDF.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.
